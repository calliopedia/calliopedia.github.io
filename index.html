Stacking Tips

Monsters and Mercenaries in Stacks

Monsters and Mercenaries should not die early in battle. They should survive long enough to perform several attacks and maximize their value.

To achieve this, their total health should remain limited so they stay protected behind your main troop stacks.

Desired Attack Order

  1. Specialists (S) attack and die first.
  2. Guardsmen (G) attack.
  3. Monsters (M) attack.
  4. Mercenaries attack last.

This allows Monsters and Mercenaries to stay alive longer and make multiple attacks before dying.

Crypt Guide

Carter and Crypt Exploration

Carter is an archaeologist captain and the specialist for crypts.

Crypt Types

  • Common (Green Oil) — Maximum level 25.
  • Rare (Blue Oil) — Maximum level 30.
  • Epic (Pink Oil) — Maximum level 35.

Sending Carter to crypts provides clan chests, captain equipment materials, and materials for Carter himself.

Improving Crypt Efficiency

  • Archaeology research.
  • Carter's equipment.
  • Torch artifact (fragments obtained from Olympus).
  • Hero talent points.

Objective 1: Carter Level 300

  • Target Epic Crypts level 25–30.
  • Keep tar cost below 35,000.
  • Use Carter together with two combat captains.

Objective 2: Omniscience Equipment

  • 3 Purple pieces.
  • 3 Orange pieces.
I chose to create only Imminent Death Daggers, so I only explored four different types of Epic Crypts.

Objective 3: Golden Amber

  • Attack Citadels.
  • Open right-facing crypts.
  • Save gems before combining them.
  • Aim directly for the highest quality possible.

Unlocking G7 Strategy

If you are ready to unlock G7, consider waiting for Ragnarok.

Ragnarok Strategy

  • Attack all available G6 monsters first.
  • Complete as much G6 content as possible.
  • Unlock G7 afterwards.

Research Priority Advice

Complete research before unlocking the next Guardsmen tier.

Recommended Research Order

  1. Riders.
  2. Melee Units.
  3. Archers.

Monster Research Considerations

Researching Monsters first may provide an advantage in Ragnarok and Great Hunt.

It may create a slight disadvantage in Dark Omens and Olympus due to different scaling rules.

Event Scaling Rules

Event Guardsmen Monsters Scaling Type
Ragnarok Yes No Fixed to Guardsmen Level
Great Hunt Yes No Fixed to Guardsmen Level
Dark Omens Yes Yes Highest of Guardsmen, Monsters, or Specialists
Olympus Yes Yes Highest of Guardsmen, Monsters, or Specialists
