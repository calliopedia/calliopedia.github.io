HEROS : If you have the Hero trials at the begginning, be sure you finish it. You have one week to complete most of the missions and open the chest. The reward of the chest is a hero of your choice!! Use this oportunity because if you want another hero later, you’ll have to buy it with 1M gold or real money.

Best Heros to start : Ayrin, Alrick, Thaddeus, Aemon. My favoride of all is Ayrin. For more information about the Heros, click here.

RESEARCH :

Although you’ll be tempted to research guardsmen as a priority, and I understand, I think the priority should be crypting first. Try to equilibrate the research on troops, crypting and boost to have an overrall growth. However, prioritise the research on crypting and army modernization will help you go farther on the long run.

Now that you read all you need to start on this game with the right foot, I’ll give you the secret of a good development. Well, it’s not a secret, you can tell everyone:

HOW TO MAXIMISE YOUR CRYPTING ( or How to crypt like a pro)

This should be everyone’s priority, as it helps the growth of the clan, as well as yours.

First step : Develop Carter. To do that, take him with Cleo or Heimdall when you have 3 captain epic monsters, like arachne or Brireus on Olympus. Or take some runs with him on Doomsday.

Save your scrolls for when Carter is on the Summon Mastery event. And get as much pieces of Carter you can, untill you can get him to 7 stars.

Get as many immortal essence you can on the immortal essence events, and when attacking epic monsters. You can see if you can get immortal essence from the epic clicking on the epic menu to see the rewards ladder.

Second : Gear. Gear is a must to improve crypting. At first when you start you’ll make the basic Tomb Raider gear. When you are able to hit citadels, start hitting cursed citadels and getting materials from epic crypts. Then you can forge the omniscience gear. Make this gear as soon as you can. Any level.

Omniscience gear : Go to the forge and look at one gear. To optimize, you can make three of the same, it doesn’t matter, all have the same bonus for the same level. Chose one, for exemple, the dagger. Look at the materials you need to forge it. Clicking on the materials, you will see on which epic crypt you find them. Go only to these crypts, and no other. Soon you’ll have enough materials to forge three good omnisicence gear.

For omniscience gear you also need master coins, these you can take destroying cursed citadels. Do it as soon as you can, to have coins to forge your gear.
