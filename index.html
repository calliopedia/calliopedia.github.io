Because Nobody Reads Clan Chat

New to the battle?

Start with this quick guide — it covers everything I wish I had known sooner.

If you’re already experienced, you can skip this part. That said, it won’t cost you anything to read it, and I’d still love your input. If you prefer, you’ll find more advanced information later on.

Heroes

If you have access to the Hero Trials at the beginning of the game, make sure you complete them. You have one week to finish most of the missions and open the chest. The reward is a hero of your choice.

Best heroes to start with: Ayrin, Alrick, Thaddeus, and Aemon. My personal favorite is Ayrin.

For more information about the heroes, click here.

Hero Bonus Guide

  • Thaddeus – Gives a boost to your captains, allowing them to take more troops per battle. An excellent choice for anyone developing their GI levels.
  • Meriones – Boosts hero strength when equipped with fully matching armor. Super useful for epics where the hero is involved. There is no boost for captains, and if the kit is not matched, the bonus does not apply.
  • Ayrin – Super useful for hopping to other kingdoms to steal silver, complete epics, and more—especially outside of KvK.
  • Alrick – Makes each attack slow down to 7.5 minutes, giving you time to empty your city or gain reinforcements. Great in KvK for holding banners as long as teammates can reinforce you.
  • Haemon – Increases clan bonus. Only works when clan bonuses are active. If your clan is not active enough to keep bonuses running, this is not worth it.

Research

It’s very tempting to focus on Guardsmen research first, and I completely understand why. Still, I believe your first priority should be crypting. Try to balance your research between troops, crypting, and boosts so your overall development stays steady. In the long run, prioritizing crypting and army modernization will take you much farther.

How to Maximize Your Crypting

How to crypt like a pro

This should be everyone’s top priority, because it helps both your own growth and the growth of the clan.

First step: Develop Carter. To do this, bring him along with Cleo or Heimdall when you have 3 captain epic monsters, such as Arachne or Briareus in Olympus. You can also take him on runs in Doomsday.

Save your scrolls for when Carter appears in the Summon Mastery event, and collect as many Carter pieces as possible until you can raise him to 7 stars.

Try to gather as much immortal essence as possible during immortal essence events and when attacking epic monsters. You can check whether an epic monster gives immortal essence by opening the epic menu and looking at the rewards ladder.

Second step: Gear. Gear is essential for improving your crypting. At the beginning, you’ll craft the basic Tomb Raider gear. Once you’re able to attack citadels, start targeting cursed citadels and collecting materials from epic crypts. That will let you forge Omniscience gear. Make this gear as soon as you can, whatever the level.

Omniscience gear: Open the forge and look at one piece of gear. To optimize your progress, you can make three copies of the same piece — it doesn’t matter which one, since they all give the same bonus at the same level. For example, you could choose the dagger. Check the materials needed to forge it. When you click on those materials, you’ll see which epic crypts provide them. Focus only on those crypts and ignore the others. Before long, you’ll have enough materials to forge three solid Omniscience pieces.

You’ll also need master coins for Omniscience gear. You can get them by destroying cursed citadels. Start doing this as soon as you can so you’ll have enough coins ready when it’s time to forge your gear.
